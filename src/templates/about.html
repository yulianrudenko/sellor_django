What is this website made for?

The main goal is to let people based in Poland to search for things they want to obtain as well as put their stuff for sale.

If you found a product you are interested in you can chat owner to talk about the deal, make an appointment to proceed with the payment.

And that's where our task is done, the rest is up to you two!

The only small detail: after the deal is successfully done you and the seller are asked to mark the deal as done on website.
